2021-2022
Title: The life & Death cycle
Description: This is supposed to be my interpretation of life and death and the cycle we go through. The drawing is divided into two halves (top and bottom), So the top portion of drawing is more defined with lines up and down, something that represents a more comprehendable 3D world we live in that has dimensions, time pushes us along an inevitable funnel towards dying (the funnel like portion in the middle), where we "cross over" throught "the eye of the universe" or die... and go to a plain of existence that perhaps is less comprehendable (the bottom half, with its more abstract patterns) and doesnt quite make as much logical sense compared to the top half. the bottom half also flows outwards and then back towards the top, hence completing the cycle of what our existence may be. the top and bottom halves are supposed to sort of mirror eachother, to represent the idea that everything that happens may have a some "meaning" both before, during and after our lifetime.
