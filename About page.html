About:

I am Jon Taylor and I love to express artwork in various mediums. My main mediums include paint, pencil, pen, ceramics, woodworking.
I dont know if I have a perticular style but I enjoy making abstract-semi abstract art emphisizing geometry and symettry. I enjoy artwork that is original, outlandish and unorthodoxed and incoroprating multiple styles and mediums within a piece is always cool to me.
I take inspiration from my life experiences, lessons, the outdoors, friends, family, memories and overall day to day interests are a few things that motivate and inspires me to create art.
