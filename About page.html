About:

My name is Jon and I like to express my artwork in various mediums. My main mediums include painting, graphite pencil, pen, ceramics, and woodworking.
I don't have a particular style, but alot of my work is orientated around making abstract too semi-abstract art, with the use of geometry and symmetry. I enjoy artwork that is original, outlandish, or unorthodox. I try to incorporate multiple styles and mediums within my artwork.
I take inspiration from my life experiences, lessons, philosophies, the outdoors, friends, family, and memories. Seeing and listening to other great artists, musicians or authors also motivates me to create art.
